About Us

Leading the way in food and beverage distribution across the globe

Who We Are

TPB-export is a company established in Macedonia by four partners who had a vision to unite the best products from Africa, and present them to the EU market. We have organized ourselves to provide these products with a passion to help stimulate economic development between these two markets while sharing their great products with our Europian friends and families from all backgrounds. Our goal will be to continue to import and represent products and companies that have high quality for the best value.

Our Mission

To be the trusted bridge between quality food and beverage producers and markets worldwide, delivering excellence in distribution, import/export, and retail services.

Our Vision

To become the leading distribution partner in the food and beverage industry, recognized for our reliability, innovation, and commitment to customer satisfaction.
